Greece does nightlife in seasons and in styles — Athens runs year-round, the islands run hard from May to September. This guide stays evergreen: it points at the right *kinds* of places in each city, then sends you to the venue pages where the facts (hours, phone, address) come from Google and the owner.

We don't fake reviews. We don't invent events. If a number is wrong, owners can claim and fix it — and the URL still belongs to the city, not the venue.
